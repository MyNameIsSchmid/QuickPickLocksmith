Residential Rekeys

Whether you've just moved in, you've lost a key, or just want to feel safer, you can rekey your locks so that they work with a different key than before. If done uncorrectly, this process could lead to many issues like "sticky" locks or nonfunctional keys. However we make sure to take the time and care to make sure your lock works even better than how we found it!

To rekey your house, our trained professional will come and make a plan with you for what locks you want rekeyed, what key you want them to match, and how long the job should take (usually around 15 minutes per lock). They will then remove the locks, take them apart, and replace the current key-pins with ones that will match your new key. Once putting everything back together, the trained professional will then reinstall the newly rekeyed lock back on the door and make sure that everything is working perfectly.

Home Lock Out

Nobody expects the worst. It's almost impossible to prepare for, and always seems to come at the worst time. When you get locked out, you have enough stress to deal with without adding on an insane locksmith cost! That's why we're here to get rid of that stress and not bust your wallet while doing it. Give us a call and we'll have a trained technician at your door at our soonest availability.

When you call us in for a lockout, our trained professional will arrive at our soonest available time. Once arrived, the service pro will make sure the lock is safe to be accessed without damaging it. Once assessed, the service pro will pick it for you and get you inside, the process usually taking no more than a couple minutes.

Sometimes it doesn't matter if a job is done sloppy. That's NOT the case when it comes to the safety of your home! Our trained professionals are committed to securing your home and making sure your locks are performing at their best. We make sure that your keys run smooth, your door shuts correctly, and that your locks are working exactly as they are meant to.

When you call us out for a lock installation or a lock repair, our trained professional comes and first spends time with you to assess exactly what needs to be done. If installing, they make sure they know exactly what goes where so as to leave you comletely satisfied. If repairing, the service pro will talk to you to know exactly what the problem is, when it started, and go over with you exactly what needs to be done to repair it before doing so. Whether repairing or installing, the service pro always leaves the fresh locks running smoothly and correctly, ensuring the most safety possible for your home.
